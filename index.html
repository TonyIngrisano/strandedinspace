Flowers in Space

Helen Sharman is AMAZING! She was the first British astronaut and the first woman to visit MIR Space Station! A chemist, Sharman accompanied Sergei Krikalev on the Soyuz TM-12 as a Research Cosmonaut at only 27 years old, and she brought along a bag of 25,000 pansy seeds to test their subjectivity to cosmic radiation. Flamingo Cosmonaut 501-639 is proud to be nicknamed after Sharman, and embarked upon her journey into outer space with a Cadmium Yellow Medium colored Ranunculus plant on her back!
